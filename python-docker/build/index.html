Spaceship

This is a spaceship. It flies through the universe, exploring new planets and encountering strange creatures.

Onboard, there are many rooms and compartments:

  • The cockpit, where the pilot sits and controls the spaceship.
  • The engine room, where engineers work to keep the engines running smoothly.
  • The living quarters, where crew members eat, sleep, and relax during their long journeys.
  • The cargo hold, where supplies and equipment are stored for the mission.

Exploring the universe is an exciting adventure, but it can also be dangerous. The spaceship must be prepared for anything that comes its way.
